Do Not Fear The Dark

Do not fear the dark,
because it's all you'll ever see from now on.

You find yourself wandering through a portal and stepping into unfamiliar territory in the form of a strange, ancient temple. But while it is unfamiliar, you recognize the all too familiar sensation of death crawling over your skin. Engraved upon the stones of the walls are words of which you cannot understand.

An overwhelming spiritual power radiates between these walls, and you desperately begin to search for a way out. However, at the sound of a low growl, you freeze. The noise grows louder and closer until you find yourself staring into the ghostly blue eyes of a Draugr. The creature snarls at you and raises its blade to strike before a deep, commanding voice causes it to yield.

"And just who would be foolish enough to step into my sanctum without an invitation?"
