Books

The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim Official Game Guide by David S. J. Hodgson and Steve Stratton (p. 645-647)

Published by US videogame strategy guide publisher Prima Games, this guide includes 3 pages on Dovahzul, sharing a key for the alphabet, some common words, and translations of word walls. Published by Prima Games in 2012, ISBN-13 978-0307895479

The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim Legendary Edition Official Game Guide by David Hodgson (p. 1096-1099)

Building on Hodgson's earlier work, this book contains 4 pages on Dovahzul, this time including further translations of word walls found in the Dawnguard/Hearthfire and Dragonborn DLCs. Published by Prima Games on 4th June 2013, ISBN-13 978-0307895509
